02
01 — Foundation
Why we exist
JEE isn't a test of how much you've read. It's a test of how precisely you perform under pressure. Most prep ignores that.
We engineered for it.
Mission
Build the most advanced adaptive prep platform ever made — one that maps every student's mind in real time and engineers the fastest possible path to mastery. Not another content library. A guidance system.
Vision
A world where any student with ambition can reach escape velocity — where the ceiling on a rank is talent and effort, never access to great teaching or the right feedback at the right moment.
Positioning statement
For serious JEE aspirants chasing a top rank,
Orange Nelumbo
Orange Nelumbo — Brand Guidelines
03
01 — Foundation
Personality
Orange Nelumbo is the engineer in the room who is also your biggest believer. Precise enough to trust with your rank, human enough to keep you going. Five traits govern every expression.
Precise
Engineered, exact, data-backed. Every claim has a number behind it. We measure what matters and say it straight.
Adaptive
Never one-size-fits-all. The platform bends to the student; so does the brand. Responsive, intelligent, alive.
Relentless
Forward motion, always. We push, iterate and never coast. The energy of a launch, not a library.
Visionary
We think in decades and light-years. Ambitious about what learning can be — and unafraid to look futuristic.
Human
Behind the telemetry is a student who is tired, hopeful and betting their future on us. We are warm, encouraging and on their side — technology in service of a person, never the other way around.

16 — Accessibility
Inclusive by default
Students study on cheap phones, on patchy data, late at night. The dark cockpit must stay legible everywhere. Target WCAG 2.1 AA.
Contrast
Body ≥ 4.5:1 on carbon. Never white on orange, never small orange body on dark. Don't rely on colour alone — pair state with a label.
Focus & targets
Visible 2px orange focus ring on every interactive element. Touch targets ≥ 44 × 44px.
Text & reading
Min 16px body. Line length 60–75 chars. Never lock zoom. Mono only for short labels & data — never long body.
Motion & media
Honour reduced-motion (no orbital sweeps), caption all video, never autoplay sound, always offer a skip.
